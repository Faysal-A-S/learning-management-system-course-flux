Who We Are:

We are a bunch of undergraduate students. Constantly trying to make things a little bit easier, just like you. The idea of CourseFlux came from facing difficulties when understanding a topic before exam night, failing to manage enough time to take preparation, looking for a friend who will help us to understand that topic which holding our confidence level for that course, hence fighting procrastination.

Our Vision:

With the mission of improving student’s lives through tutoring academic courses, CourseFlux is a Bangladeshi open online undergraduate course provider aimed at professional adults and university students. On this platform, you can find your undergraduate program course lectures individually for every university in Bangladesh. The content creator of those lectures, topics will be your varsity mate to make sure you can get a comprehensible idea of your chosen course according to your varsity curriculum.

WHO ARE WE TRYING TO REACH:

Since university students are diverse rather than school-college students, a big portion of them are already maintaining other things besides education. Some of them are professional artists, interns, part-time workers, businessmen/ businesswomen, some are even married, some of them are already raising kids besides completing their graduation. So managing enough time to participate in everyday lectures and self-study becomes tough for them. Hence CourseFlux is designed to help those undergraduate students to cover their courses before exams by providing lectures according to their university syllabus and prepare them for the exams of tomorrow.

WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR:

If you think you mastered some of your undergraduate courses or if you are that person, whom everyone seeks before exams to understand any difficult topic. Then this is the perfect platform for you. CourseFlux always welcomes lecturers/content creators. Here you can join as a lecturer/course content creator, earn money alongside helping your friends, juniors by selling them your course lectures. Because we believe that, the amount of time, hardships, patience a student spends to master a subject or course and then share with others should be valued.
